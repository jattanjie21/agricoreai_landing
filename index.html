Overview

The Intelligence at the Heart of Farming

AgriCore AI is a global agricultural intelligence platform designed to help farmers make better decisions using AI, real-time data, and localized guidance.

The Problem

Farmers worldwide struggle with low yields, climate uncertainty, pest outbreaks, poor market access, and limited technical knowledge. Many rely on guesswork, leading to low income and risks of crop failure.

820M Undernourished
70% Smallholder Farmers

Our Solution

AgriCore AI leverages artificial intelligence to provide smart crop and livestock recommendations, soil & climate-based guidance, AI-powered pest detection, market prices, financial tools, irrigation management, and comprehensive agriculture education.
